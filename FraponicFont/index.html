Fraponic Font

This is the website for downloading the Fraponic Font. This is used for Besonasige style lettering.

  RUH SIARO ISI ERANIGE.
  ruh siaro isi eranige.
  
  RUH ERUESI ISI ENERU.
  ruh eruesi isi eneru.
  
  MES FRERE AVOIR UN ROUGE CHAPEAU.
  mes frere avoir un rouge chapeau.

Note that capital letters are in Hafestran script, and lowercase is all capitals. Eventually going to include proper punctuation as time allows.
